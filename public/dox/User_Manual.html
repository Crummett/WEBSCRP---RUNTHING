Starting Up

Once the website is installed, browse to the ip followed by ‘:8080’.

Users

Start by creating a user. Click on ‘Profile’ in the top navigation bar.

Fill out the form and press ‘Create’.

Your new profile will be created and can be found as a member of the ‘Unattached’ club.

Therefore, to find your profile click ‘Clubs’ at the top to get the full club directory and click ‘Unattached’ and then ‘Club Members’ and click on your newly created user.

To edit the user, click ‘Update profile’, change the form, then click ‘Post’.

Clubs

To create a club, click ‘Clubs’ at the top, then ‘Create a new club’.

Fill out the form then press ‘Create’.

Click ‘Clubs’ at the top, and then select your new club from the list to load your club profile.

Click ‘Show Map’ to load a Google Maps widget showing the address you put in.

To add your profile to the club, select the correct name in the dropdown menu then click ‘Request to Join’.

To accept the request click ‘(Join Requests)’ and then either ‘Accept’ or ‘Deny’ next to the relevant name.

Back on the club’s page, you can also post to the club’s wall, by typing in the form at the bottom, selecting the poster from the dropdown menu, and clicking ‘Post’.

Events

To create an event, click ‘Events’ at the top, then ‘Create a new event’.

Fill out the form then press ‘Create’.

Click ‘Events’ at the top, and then select your new event from the list.

Click ‘Show Map’ to load a Google Maps widget showing the address you put in.

To add results, select the user from the dropdown menu, enter their time, and click ‘Submit’.

To edit the user, click ‘Update event’, change the form, then click ‘Update’.
